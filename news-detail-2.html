China Quality Service Successfully Passes ISO 9001 Annual Review

Published: 2026-05-28 Views: 986 Source: China Quality Service

After rigorous audit assessment, China Quality Service has once again successfully passed the ISO 9001:2015 quality management system annual review. This demonstrates the company's continued international leadership in quality management system development.

The Audit Process

This annual review was conducted by an internationally recognized certification body, covering all business processes including inspection service management, client communication, supplier management, document control, and internal auditing. The audit team highly praised the company's quality management system.

Client Value

ISO 9001 certification is an internationally recognized quality management standard. Passing the annual review not only validates the standardization and reliability of our service processes but also provides clients with confidence in choosing China Quality Service as their product inspection service partner.

Continuous Improvement

China Quality Service has always made quality management system development a core strategy. Through regular internal audits, management reviews, and customer satisfaction surveys, we continuously optimize service processes and improve service quality.
